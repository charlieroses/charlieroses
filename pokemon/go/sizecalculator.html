This is a work in progress. Still cleaning some stuff up. Just wanted to get this online so I could test it out on a walk.

The tool is accurate in that it's gonna get you an accurate range. It's not possible to get an exact number. The actual height and weight of Pokemon displayed in Pokemon Go are rounded meaning a Bulbasaur that weighs 5.18 kg can weigh anywhere between 5.175 kg and 5.185 kg. Same applies for height and showcase scores.

Showcase scores get rounded to the nearest whole number. I have noticed in some places, the showcase score for a Pokemon appears to vary. I have an XXL Ducklett (8.88kg,0.84m) in a Ducklett showcase that has 1082 points. In the standings view and in the select pokemon view, it shows 1082 points. In the "Your Ducklet" view, it shows 1081 points. I think it's just a bug in how the points are displayed. I'm assuming that my Ducklett has a score somewhere in the range of 1081.5 and 1081.9, which is getting rounded up to 1082 for the showcase, but this display is chopping off the fractional component instead of rounding it.

IVs are only necessary to calculate possible showcase scores. Currently, this tool only predicts species specific showcases. I might expand it to include other showcases.
