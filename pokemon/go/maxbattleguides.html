Max Battle Team Builder

Most max battle and raid guides typically only list the top counters for each boss. For raids, if you don't have any of the good counters, it's less an issue because you can rejoin the battle and zerg rush the boss. For max battles where you only get 3 pokemon, it can be a bit of a problem. Strong Dynamax pokemon are also extremely expensive and some have extremely limited availability, like Gigantamax Lapras who was only available for 3 hours.

Each guide lists out every available Dynamax Pokemon and how they stack up against each Max Battle boss so that you can build the best team possible based on the Pokemon and resources that you have available.
